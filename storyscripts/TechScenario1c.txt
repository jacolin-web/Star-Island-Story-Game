Bryce decides that she wants to get more women involved in Civic Tech, and starts her own Civic Tech Group, inviting women Civic Technologists to become involved, and then reaching out to Women Politicians either politically representing the topic or interested in representing it. This is all well and good, since it gets more women technologists involved in Civic Tech, and more women politicians representing their interests. However, she doesn't manage to break through the old boys' network. In the end she hasn't really changed anything. She hasn't managed to changed men's minds regarding women in Civic Tech and representing their interests. Patience 1, Confidence 1, Networking 2, Male Awareness 1
