Bryce enters politics. She lands a position in the White working closely with the oval office. However, she finds out early on that she wasn't chosen for the job because of her skills or experience. The President has had his eyes on her since she came in for an interview. He tells her that she can keep her position only if she consents to have an affair with him. Bryce has a big, weighty decision to make. Does she consent and "purportedly" seal her golden future by having an affair with the president? Or does she turn him down and risk losing her position? Or Maybe Bryce wants to keep her position, but she doesn't want to get involved in an affair with the President?
