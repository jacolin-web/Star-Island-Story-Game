Welcome to Star Island! Click Start Game to play!

How to play: 

Just click on 'START GAME' and you will be transported to Star Island! 
You will visit the five different islands and solve issues within each community. 
You will gain new experience and various tokens once you complete each island 
These new skills will allow you to defeat the boss in the final island 
Focus on creating better environments for the civilians. Good luck!
